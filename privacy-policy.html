CycleTrace Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: July, 2025

1. Information We Collect

CycleTrace collects the following types of information:

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates during your cycling activities
  • Activity Data: Distance, speed, duration, and route information
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: App features used and interaction patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Track and record your cycling activities
  • Provide route mapping and navigation features
  • Generate statistics and performance metrics
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local storage with device encryption
  • Secure data transmission when syncing
  • Regular security updates and patches

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and safety

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of data collection

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for:

  • Maps and navigation (OpenStreetMap)
  • Weather information
  • Analytics and crash reporting

These services have their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

CycleTrace is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

9. Contact Us

Email: privacy@cycletrace.app
